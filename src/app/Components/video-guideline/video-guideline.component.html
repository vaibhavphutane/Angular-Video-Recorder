Video Guideline
• Seat in front of a window or a light source to ensure face is well lit.
• Keep sufficient distance from the camera. Do not seat too close or too far away from the camera
• Make sure you are the only person recording the video. No other person should be visible during the recording
• Avoid use of dark shades (goggles) while recording the video
• Preview the recorded video before submitting. If you want to re-record, Refresh the webpage and start again
